Who We Are

We are expert in Web Development, WordPress Development, IT Consulting, Web Maintenance & revamping.

We are young and energetic tech savvies with a hunger for latest innovation on the web. We apply all the technology evolution strategies in delevering unique projects.

Information Gathering & Planning

The first step in designing a successful web site is to gather information. This first step is actually the most important one, as it involves a solid understanding of the client's need. It involves a good understanding of you – what your business goals and dreams are, and how the web can be utilized to help you achieve those goals.
Things we consider are Purpose, Goals, Target Audience & Content.

Design & Development

From the information gathered up to this point, it’s time to determine the look and feel of your site.
As part of the design phase, we incorporate elements such as the company logo or colors to help strengthen the identity of your company on the web site. We create prototype designs for your web site either sent via email with the mock-ups for your web site or giving you access to a secure area of your web site meant for customers to view work in progress.

Testing & Delivery

At this point, we attend to the final details and test your web site. We test things such as the complete functionality of forms or other scripts, as well last testing for last minute compatibility issues (viewing differences between different web browsers), ensuring that your web site is optimized to be viewed properly in the most recent browser versions.

Maintenance

It’s really up to you as far as how comfortable you feel with updating your own web site. Some people prefer to have all the control so that they can make updates to their own web site the minute they decide to do so. Others prefer to hand off the web site entirely, as they have enough tasks on-hand that are more important for them to handle directly. That’s where we once again come in, once again, as we can take over the web site maintenance for you – one less thing for you to do is always a good thing in these busy times!

Web Design & Development

Social Media Marketing

Content Writing & Development

WordPress Development

At Lighthouse Media we have years of experience in WordPress Development. As we all know, wordpress is known and reputed all over the globe as an open source content management system that is based upon the PHP language. However, for us, it’s not about just nominally designing and developing a wordpress website for any client. We are interested in providing ‘more for less’. No matter if it’s a business website, blog website or a well-arranged Ecommerce website, we are well reputed for delivering any project on appropriate time, which can also reduce your concerns.

Website Maintenance and Support

We believe that the ongoing technical support, content management, optimization and marketing of your website are paramount to its continued success. Keeping your website up to date with fresh content, new products, promotions are just some ways to keep your website performing above your competitors. WebWorks is our service for clients who require a formal arrangement to fulfil their web support and maintenance needs; ensuring that your online web investment is protected and that your web site(s) evolve to meet existing and future customer demands.
